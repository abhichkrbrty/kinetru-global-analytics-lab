Business Analyst & Strategy Track

Role Objective

Support pre-MVP and research direction through structured thinking, surveys, and strategy artifacts.

This role is non-coding. The focus is on clarity, validation, and decision support.

⸻

Core Skills
	•	Survey design & analysis
	•	User personas & Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD)
	•	Competitive and market analysis
	•	Clear documentation & slide decks

⸻

Week 1 – Problem Framing

Focus: Understanding what we are building and for whom

Tasks:
	•	Write a 1-page concept note for Kinetru Global
	•	Draft Survey v1 (10–12 questions)
	•	Create 2 initial personas

Deliverable:
	•	Concept note (PDF/MD)
	•	Survey v1 document
	•	Persona summaries

⸻

Week 2 – Validation & Requirements

Focus: Turning ideas into requirements

Tasks:
	•	Refine survey based on feedback
	•	Conduct competitive scan (5–8 tools)
	•	Draft MVP requirements v1

Deliverable:
	•	Competitive analysis document
	•	MVP requirements v1

⸻

Week 3 – Strategy & Synthesis

Focus: Making insights actionable

Tasks:
	•	Summarize survey insights
	•	Write an insights memo (1–2 pages)
	•	Prepare a short strategy deck

Deliverable:
	•	Insights memo
	•	Strategy deck (10–12 slides)

⸻

Recommended Study Topics
	•	Survey bias & question design
	•	JTBD framework
	•	MVP requirement writing
	•	Competitive analysis frameworks
